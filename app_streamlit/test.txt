HINT 모델 테스트용 샘플 데이터
- Phase3
- Diseases: Chronic Obstructive Pulmonary Disease (COPD) 
- ICD-10 Codes: J44.9 (상세불명의 만성 폐쇄성 폐질환)
- Drugs: Roflumilast
- SMILES: CONC(=O)C1=CC(=C(C=C1)OCC(F)F)OC2=C(C=CC=N2)Cl
- Protocol Criteria
- Inclusion Criteria: 
    - Patients must be 40 to 75 years of age.
    - Diagnosis of COPD for at least 12 months.
    - Post-bronchodilator FEV1/FVC ratio < 0.70.
    - History of at least two clinical exacerbations of COPD within the previous year.

Exclusion Criteria:
    - Patients with a diagnosis of asthma or lung cancer.
    - Current use of other phosphodiesterase-4 inhibitors.
    - Known hypersensitivity to roflumilast or any of its excipients.
    - Severe hepatic impairment (Child-Pugh B or C).
